In today’s real wedding segment, we share details from V & V’s Indian wedding including a fun-filled Sangeet, beautiful Hindu ceremony, and a night to remember reception. P.S. Eventful provided Month/Day-of Coordination Services for the three events.

About The Ceremony

450 guests gathered at Apollo Convention Centre for the couple’s Hindu Ceremony. The day kicked off with a traditional energetic Baraat where the bride’s family welcomed the groom’s side.

About The Reception

800 guests attended the couple’s reception at Apollo Convention Centre in Mississauga which was decorated with beautiful white structures and touches of gold and pink. Highlights included a customized dance floor, an interactive emcee, customized glow sticks, and aerial dancers pouring champagne for guests.

P.S. Eventful's Planning

P.S. Eventful provided Month/Day of Wedding Coordination for V&V for three events (sangeet, ceremony and reception). Our role included creating a detailed itinerary for each event, managing vendors throughout the day, ensuring each event ran on time and flowed seamlessly. Our aim was to ensure the couple and their families did not have to worry about a thing!
